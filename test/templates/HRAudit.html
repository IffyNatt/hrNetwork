HR NETwork becomes your virtual Human Resource Department, offering expert guidance for day-to-day inquiries on employment related matters. Your HR Advisor package includes:

HR Audit Compliance

HR NETwork will come on-site to conduct a comprehensive assessment and audit, reviewing all areas of HR compliance required of employers including: employee file set up, compliance and maintenance; procedures and policies for employee issues; affirmative action plan; safety plan; wage and hour compliance; hiring and termination practices; payroll procedure; required postings; etc.... After the audit, you will receive a full report highlighting areas of concern regarding compliance issues with an analysis and recommendations, as well as a timeline of proposed changes.
